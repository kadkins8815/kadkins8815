About

Hi, I'm Keith. I was born and raised in South Bend, Indiana (and of course a huge Notre Dame fan!) After graduating High School I had no clue what I wanted to do and after several knick knack jobs I somehow found myself in the Hospitality industry working at the Marriott in South Bend as a Bellman. What was even better was that this was the hotel that housed all Notre Dame football oppentents. What a great gig for a die hard ND fan... Anyway, over the next 20 years I was promoted to various Mangemement positions held within Marriott and Hilton branded hotels, most recetly holding the title as a Area General Manager---until--- the pandemic hit and was laid off. So as many are doing during this time I have had the priviledge to take a step back and re-evualte myself. I cannot wait to unlock this new potential and enthusiam to see what my future holds.
